Digital Literature Distribution

Enables wholesalers, advisors, and agents with the ability to obtain tailored financial product literature and forms in their preferred distribution method, with a digital first focus.

Preferences and time-to-market needs are changing to digital and on-demand solutions. In an industry predominately dependent on distributing printed materials, there needs to be solutions with a digital first focus. Providing cost effective and timely alternatives are more important than ever for financial planners as they provide guidance and advice to consumers.

Drivers:

#1 request from wholesalers, advisors, agents -> “Provide digital distribution of financial product kits.”

  • Access is immediate vs waiting for shipment of physical kits
  • Allows for pre-meeting planning with clients to help tailor offerings
  • Advisors and consumers feel the standard kit, as it is delivered today, is comprehensive but also overwhelming
  • Advisors admit they "pitch" most of the material shipped to them as they are only looking for key components until decisions are made to purchase products
Reduce print and shipping costs
  • Over a 12-month period over 550,000 physical kits are ordered
  • The cost to get a kit into the advisors hands ranges from $6.76 up to $18.50 for print and fulfillment and $7 - $10 for shipping.
  • High number of orders are sent overnight to accommodate advisor needs for meetings scheduled with clients
Savings opportunities include:

Monetary:

  • A conservative estimate of the lowest price kit, at a 5% rate for digital over print distribution, the first year savings would exceed $207,000.
